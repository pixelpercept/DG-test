Scotoma Mapping Tool

This experimental tool helps you map visual blind spots using dynamic noise.

Instructions

  • Use one eye at a time (cover the other)
  • Keep your gaze fixed on the center cross
  • Click where you perceive distortions, missing or unstable areas
  • Maintain a consistent viewing distance

What you may notice

  • Central blind spot: missing or altered area near fixation
  • Distortions: noise appears warped or unstable
  • Faded zones: reduced contrast or flickering perception



You can export your results and repeat the test over time to track changes.

For consistency, use the same screen, distance, and test conditions.

You may share the exported file with your eye specialist if needed.

This is not a medical diagnostic tool.
